MAGNUM OPUS

Magnum Opus is an artistic and experimental project that merges ceramic research with alchemical symbolism. The work consists of a vertical cabinet divided into four layers, each representing one of the traditional stages of alchemy: Nigredo, Albedo, Citrinitas, and Rubedo.

SEVEN STAGES OF ENLIGHTENMENT

Seven Stages of Enlightenment is a series of seven porcelain spheres that investigates transformation through material and symbol. Each sphere is defined by a distinct glaze, developed through ongoing research into ceramic glaze chemistry. Rooted in the alchemical stages of the Magnum Opus, the work brings together technical experimentation and metaphoric structure, presenting glaze not only as finish, but as evidence of change.

ALCHEMYST IN PROGRESS

AlchemYst in Progress is a living sculpture composed of a sphere of soil from which three plants emerge. Drawing on the alchemical stages of Nigredo, Albedo, and Rubedo, the work frames transformation as a biological process, rooted in matter, growth, and time.

MEDITATION

Meditation is a mixed-media installation structured around a simple axis: a low wooden table set against a white wall, a glass chalice filled with raw honey, and a sand-filled cushion placed on the ground for kneeling or sitting. The work invites the viewer into a physical posture of pause, where stillness becomes a deliberate act.

ENLIGHTENMENT

Enlightenment is an immersive installation centered on a large black sphere containing a column of warm light. Inspired initially by astrophysics and later reframed through the experience of aphantasia, the work connects cosmic phenomena with the structure of thought, inviting the viewer to move physically from exterior reality into an inner, conceptual space.
