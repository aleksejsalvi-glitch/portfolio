CERAMICS

This section gathers two complementary practices: the analytical mapping of glaze surfaces through test bowls and the direct shaping of clay by hand. The bowls register transformation through fire within contained form; the modeled pieces carry that transformation into space. Together they articulate a sustained inquiry into material change..
